---
title: "Ceaseless Skip"
---

The **Ceaseless Skip** is a [sequence break](/sequence-break) in [Dark Souls](/darksouls) that allows the player to skip [Ceaseless Discharge](https://darksouls.wikidot.com/ceaseless-discharge) by rolling through lava in the [Demon Ruins](https://darksouls.wikidot.com/demon-ruins). It saves about 2:00 minutes.
